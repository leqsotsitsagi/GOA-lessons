About GOA(Goal Oriented Academy)

GOA (Goal Oriented Academy) is a leading programming and IT skills training institute based in Tbilisi, Georgia. Recognized as one of the top programming academies in Europe,
GOA offers a diverse range of courses aimed at equipping students with practical, real-world skills necessary for the modern job market.

Key features of GOA:

  1. Comprehensive Curriculum:
    - GOA provides a variety of courses, including front-end and back-end development, data analysis, web development, UX/UI design, and QA testing.
    - The curriculum is designed to cover both foundational and advanced topics in programming languages such as Python, JavaScript, and SQL.

  2. Practical Training:
    - Emphasis is placed on hands-on training and real-world application. Students work on projects that mirror actual industry challenges,
    ensuring they gain relevant and practical experience.

  3. Expert Faculty:
    - Courses are taught by experienced professionals and industry experts, providing students with valuable insights and knowledge from those actively working in the field.

  4. Career Support:
    - GOA offers robust career support services, including job placement assistance and partnerships with tech companies.
    This helps ensure that graduates have a smooth transition into the workforce and can find employment in their chosen field.

  5. Reputation and Recognition:
    - The academy has garnered a strong reputation not only in Georgia but across Europe, being recognized for its high-quality education and successful training programs.

Summary:

GOA is dedicated to fostering a conducive learning environment that bridges the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application.
With its extensive course offerings, skilled faculty, and strong industry connections, GOA stands out as a premier destination for individuals looking to advance their careers in programming and IT.

Currently there are about 1400 students learning in GOA 30 teachers that teach programming and 60 leaders that will keep their eyes on how you study.
